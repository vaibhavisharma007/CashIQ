MoneyIQ Financial Advisor App Support

Getting Started

To get started with MoneyIQ, download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store and create an account.

FAQs

  • How do I create an account?
  • How do I link my bank accounts?
  • How do I set up my financial goals?
  • How do I get support?

How do I create an account?

To create an account, download the MoneyIQ app from the App Store or Google Play Store and follow the on-screen instructions. You will need to provide some basic personal information and create a username and password.

How do I link my bank accounts?

To link your bank accounts, go to the "Accounts" tab in the MoneyIQ app and click on "Link Accounts". Follow the on-screen instructions to search for and select your bank. You will then be prompted to enter your online banking credentials to link your account.

How do I set up my financial goals?

To set up your financial goals, go to the "Goals" tab in the MoneyIQ app and click on "Create Goal". Follow the on-screen instructions to enter your goal details and set a target date and amount. MoneyIQ will then provide you with a personalized plan to help you achieve your goal.

How do I get support?

If you need further assistance, you can contact MoneyIQ support by emailing [support@moneyiq.com](mailto:support@moneyiq.com) or calling (888) 123-4567.
